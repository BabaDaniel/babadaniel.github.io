Otaru Otaru & Co

Website Developer • February 2019

Otaru Otaru & Co is one of Nigeria’s leading law firm providing the highest standard of representation in all spheres of legal practice to clients in Nigeria and around the world. With over 25 years of experience in the legal practice, the firm is also in the forefront of keeping itself abreast with new developments in the field of law and practice. I collaborated with the in-house team to style the website using the colors from the existing logo and trademark. The brief from the in-house team highlighted the need for a content management system, carefully designed with a qualitative and more user-friendly interface on the “who we are”, “what we do”, “careers and publications pages”. Corporate websites don’t prioritize using hi-res images, but I believe one picture is greater than a thousand words. So we utilized stock images and complimented them with the appropriate text. I actually enjoyed the project with Otaru Otaru & Co Law Firm, they helped me with prompt and constructive feedback which smoothened implementation. The Otaru Otaru & Co website now has a more user-friendly look and a responsive design. At the end of the project, I went over to their office to train them on how to easily update content into the website, a process which was once hectic has been made simple.
